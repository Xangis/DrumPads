DrumPads

DrumPads is an application that allows you to use the mouse, keyboard, or touchscreen to play virtual drum pads and create beats.

It also allows you to control external MIDI devices and can also be controlled by external MIDI devices such as a Korg NanoPad.

It can be controlled with the keyboard and is designed to let you play the drums two-handed, with the QWER and TYUI keys controlling pads 1-4, ASDF and GHJK keys controlling pads 5-8, and ZXCV and BNM, keys controlling pads 9-12.

The "MIDI Settings" button lets you choose which MIDI devices to use for input and output and on what channel. MIDI output is disabled by default, so you'll need to click that button if you want to control external devices.

The following drums are included:

  • 22 Kick Drum
  • 14 Snare Drum
  • 2 Floor Tom
  • 4 Low Tom
  • 6 Mid Tom
  • 4 Hi Tom
  • 6 Closed Hi-Hat
  • 6 Open Hi-Hat
  • 5 Ride Cymbal
  • 6 Crash Cymbal
  • 1 Hand Drum
  • 2 Bongo
  • 2 Clap
  • 3 Cowbell
  • 1 Doumbek
  • 3 Timpani and Kettle Drum
  • 2 Woodblock
  • 1 Gong
  • 10 Other percussion sounds

You can use the left and right arrows on each pad to cycle through the different samples available. If you find a set of pads you like, you can save the drum kit to be loaded later. You can also lock the pads to prevent accidentally switching drum sounds during a performance.
